About Dignans Catering Service

With over 30 years experience in the catering industry, Dignans Catering are one of the leading outside catering specialists in Lanarkshire.

Our Head Chef, Meleni Larkins is a professional chef with many years experience and along with a team that also shares his passion to provide you with a truly professional service from start to finish, we will cater your event with the best quality cuisine. Whatever the occasion - be it a wedding, corporate event, birthday or special celebration or funeral, Dignans Catering can help create the perfect event and menu for you. Our menus and services can all be individually tailored to suit your exact requirements, including any special dietary requirements.
